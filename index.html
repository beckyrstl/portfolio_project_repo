About

For the past 15 years, I've worked in nonprofit communications and management, currently serving as Director of Marketing and Communications for FOCUS St. Louis.

Managing website content has always been a part of my jobs, and along the way, I've picked up elements of website design and coding. I joined the CoderGirl website design and development cohort in January 2018 in order to gain a deeper understanding of the fundamentals and principles of website development.

I look forward to using the knowledge and skills I'm gaining in my everyday work, as well as expanding my future career opportunities.
